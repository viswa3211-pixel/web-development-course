ABOUT CHILDERNS DAY

HAPPY CHILDERNS DAY

Children's Day

Is a special day for children
Children's Day is celebrated on 
14 November
Every year the birthday of the first prime minister of India Jawaharlal Nehru, who was known to have been fond of children. The day is a reminder of the importance of child welfare, education, and rights, with schools and communities organizing fun activities, cultural programs, and games to celebrate the joy of childhood.

AIM OF THE CHILDERNS DAY

The aim of Children's Day is to honor children, raise awareness about their rights and welfare, and recognize their importance to society's future. It emphasizes the need to provide every child with love, respect, and opportunities for education, health, and overall well-being.
